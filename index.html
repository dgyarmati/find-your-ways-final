Our tours

Entry Level

A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step – the essentials of Budapest every visitor has to know. General orientation on our history, culture, everyday life, dos and don'ts in 2 to 4 hours.

Sights featured: Parliament, Buda Castle, Fisherman's Bastion, Market hall, Heroes square, St Stephen's Basilica, and many more.

Mysterious 7 – Design above all

In the 7th district art is literally all around. Murals make the walls colorful. Boutique stores everywhere filled with indie designers' goods, clothes, accessories. It doesn’t matter if you prefer vintage items or highest quality products money can buy - here you’ll find something that steals your heart.

After a walk in this exciting neighborhood a ruin bar with its unique atmosphere is the best place to recharge your batteries. We finish the tour in a silkscreening shrine where you can learn and try the basics of silk screening on zero waste materials, and take home the wearables you created.
